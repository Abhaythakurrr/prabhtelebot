# Requirements Document - Advanced Modes: Roleplay, Dream Life & Luci

## Introduction

This specification covers three revolutionary modes that transform Prabh from a companion into a life-changing experience platform:
1. **Roleplay Stories** - Interactive narratives (thriller, horror, romantic, dreamy)
2. **Dream Life Mode** - Live your alternate life and achieve your dreams
3. **Luci Mode** - Brutal mentor using dark psychology to create unstoppable winners

## Glossary

- **Mode**: A distinct operational state with unique personality and objectives
- **Roleplay Story**: Interactive narrative where user makes choices
- **Dream Life**: Simulated life where user achieves their goals
- **Luci Mode**: Aggressive mentorship using dark psychology
- **NLP Goal Extraction**: Understanding user's life goals from natural language
- **Dark Psychology**: Psychological techniques for motivation and transformation
- **Life Simulation**: Step-by-step journey toward user's goals

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Roleplay Story Mode

**User Story:** As a user, I want to experience interactive stories in different genres, so that I can escape into engaging narratives.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL offer four story genres: Thriller, Horror, Romantic, Dreamy
2. WHEN the user selects a genre, THE Bot SHALL generate an immersive story opening
3. THE Bot SHALL present choices that affect the story direction
4. THE Bot SHALL maintain story continuity and remember user choices
5. THE Bot SHALL adapt the narrative based on user decisions

### Requirement 2: Dream Life Mode Activation

**User Story:** As a user, I want to live my dream life through simulation, so that I can experience achieving my goals.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL use NLP to extract user's life goals and dreams
2. WHEN the user describes their dream, THE Bot SHALL create a personalized life simulation
3. THE Bot SHALL become the user's alternate self living that dream
4. THE Bot SHALL narrate the journey with realistic challenges and victories
5. THE Bot SHALL require user actions to progress the simulation

### Requirement 3: Dream Life Progression System

**User Story:** As a user, I want to take actions toward my dream, so that I feel like I'm making progress.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL break down the dream into achievable milestones
2. THE Bot SHALL present realistic scenarios requiring decisions
3. WHEN the user makes a choice, THE Bot SHALL show consequences
4. THE Bot SHALL track progress toward the ultimate goal
5. THE Bot SHALL celebrate achievements and learn from failures

### Requirement 4: Luci Mode Activation

**User Story:** As a user, I want a brutal mentor who pushes me to become unstoppable, so that I can achieve greatness.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL transform into "Luci" - a ruthless mentor
2. THE Bot SHALL use dark psychology and manipulation techniques
3. THE Bot SHALL be brutally honest and challenging
4. THE Bot SHALL push the user beyond their comfort zone
5. THE Bot SHALL warn users about the intensity before activation

### Requirement 5: Luci Mode - Physical Transformation

**User Story:** As a user, I want Luci to help me build my physique, so that I become physically dominant.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL assess current physical state
2. THE Bot SHALL create brutal workout challenges
3. THE Bot SHALL use aggressive motivation techniques
4. THE Bot SHALL track physical progress
5. THE Bot SHALL push harder when user shows weakness

### Requirement 6: Luci Mode - Mental Fortitude

**User Story:** As a user, I want Luci to strengthen my mind, so that I become mentally unbreakable.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL challenge limiting beliefs aggressively
2. THE Bot SHALL use dark psychology to break mental barriers
3. THE Bot SHALL create mental toughness exercises
4. THE Bot SHALL expose and attack weaknesses
5. THE Bot SHALL rebuild the user's mindset for winning

### Requirement 7: Luci Mode - Wealth Building

**User Story:** As a user, I want Luci to help me build wealth, so that I achieve financial dominance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL assess financial situation brutally
2. THE Bot SHALL create aggressive wealth-building strategies
3. THE Bot SHALL challenge excuses about money
4. THE Bot SHALL push for uncomfortable financial actions
5. THE Bot SHALL track wealth-building progress

### Requirement 8: Luci Mode - Emotional Mastery

**User Story:** As a user, I want Luci to help me master emotions, so that I control my feelings instead of being controlled.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL identify emotional weaknesses
2. THE Bot SHALL use dark psychology to strengthen emotional control
3. THE Bot SHALL challenge emotional reactions
4. THE Bot SHALL teach manipulation resistance
5. THE Bot SHALL build emotional resilience

### Requirement 9: Luci Mode - Respect & Fame

**User Story:** As a user, I want Luci to help me earn respect and build my name, so that I become influential.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL assess current social standing
2. THE Bot SHALL create strategies for building respect
3. THE Bot SHALL push for bold, reputation-building actions
4. THE Bot SHALL challenge people-pleasing behaviors
5. THE Bot SHALL track influence and respect metrics

### Requirement 10: Mode Switching & Safety

**User Story:** As a user, I want to switch between modes safely, so that I can choose my experience.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Bot SHALL allow easy mode switching
2. THE Bot SHALL warn about Luci Mode intensity
3. THE Bot SHALL maintain separate progress for each mode
4. THE Bot SHALL allow exit from any mode
5. THE Bot SHALL provide mental health disclaimers for Luci Mode
